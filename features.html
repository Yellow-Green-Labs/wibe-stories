Speak

Speak naturally. We turn it into a card.

Record with your mic

Your voice attaches to the card. Shared cards include a play button; downloads become an animated video with waveform.

44 languages + auto-detect

Speak in your language. The app detects it automatically. Pick from 44 languages or let the engine figure it out.

Two speech engines

Deepgram Nova-3 and Web Speech API work together for accuracy. If one fails, the other takes over.

Whisper-friendly

Speak softly, in a coffee shop, or in a quiet room. The mic picks you up without shouting.

Write

Type when speaking isn’t an option.

Type directly

No mic needed. Open the text box and type your message. All tone and style options still apply.

2

Up to 150 characters

Short messages read best on cards. If you hit 150 mid-word, the Grace zone lets you finish it — up to 160.

3

No account needed

Open the page and start typing. Your card is ready in seconds. No sign-up, no credit card.

4

Paste from anywhere

Copy a message from WhatsApp, notes, or email and paste it straight into the text box. Works in any language.

Rewrite

Find the right words in one tap.

6 AI tone styles

Warm, professional, poetic, enthusiastic, concise, heartfelt. Pick a tone and the AI rewrites your message.

Warm Professional Poetic Enthusiastic Concise Heartfelt

Free: 5 per tone per day

Free users get 5 rewrites per tone per day. Enough for everyday messages, birthday wishes, and quick notes.

Pro: unlimited rewrites

Pro users get unlimited tone rewrites with no daily caps. Plus 30-second recordings and 50 cards per day.

Labels update automatically

Voice + tone = “Voice Styled.” Text + tone = “Story Styled.” The card label always reflects how it was made.

Design

Make every card yours.

Color palettes

Choose from curated palettes that set the card background. Pick what fits the mood — warm, calm, bold, or soft.

Corner shapes

Sharp corners for messaging apps. Rounded corners for Instagram or gallery-style sharing. Toggle freely.

Font sizes

Bump the card text up for short messages. Keep it compact for longer ones. The card adjusts to fit.

Occasion images + script fonts

53 occasion backgrounds for birthdays, anniversaries, festivals. Script fonts for Telugu, Hindi, Kannada, and more.

Share

Share the feeling, not just the words.

Share as link

Get a shareable link that lasts 36 hours. Opens as a beautiful landing page with the card, voice, and optional play button.

Download as PNG

Download the card as a PNG image. Stays on your device forever. Share it anywhere, anytime.

WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, X

Share directly to messaging apps and social platforms. Native share API support for iOS, Android, and desktop.

Voice plays inline

Cards with voice include a play button. Recipients hear your voice on the landing page without downloading anything.

Feature questions

“Native” is for languages that the speech-to-text engines don’t specifically name but can still auto-detect. If your language isn’t listed individually, select Native and the engine will do its best with automatic detection. The card label will display “Native” instead of a specific language name.

Yes, completely free. No account or credit card needed. Free users get 15-second audio recordings (up to 5 per day) and 5 AI rewrites per tone per day.

Pro unlocks unlimited tone rewrites with no daily caps, 30-second audio recordings (up to 50 per day), and priority support. Upgrade directly from the app.

The textarea accepts up to 150 characters for the best-looking cards. If you reach 150 with a word still in progress, the system lets you finish it — up to 160 characters. That extra space is the Grace zone. You will see the counter turn red and show “(Grace)” as a reminder to wrap up.

Shared links last 36 hours. The card image and metadata are stored temporarily. Download the card as a PNG if you want to keep it forever.

When a shared link expires, the landing page is no longer accessible. You can always create a new card with the same message. Downloaded PNGs stay on your device permanently.

Shared card links expire automatically after 36 hours. There is no manual delete button because card data is already temporary. If you need something removed sooner, email us.
