Feature questions

“Native” is for languages that the speech-to-text engines don’t specifically name but can still auto-detect. If your language isn’t listed individually, select Native and the engine will do its best with automatic detection. The card label will display “Native” instead of a specific language name.

Yes, completely free. No account or credit card needed. Free users get 15-second audio recordings (up to 5 per day) and 5 AI rewrites per tone per day.

Pro unlocks unlimited tone rewrites with no daily caps, 30-second audio recordings (up to 50 per day), and priority support. Upgrade directly from the app.

The textarea accepts up to 150 characters for the best-looking cards. If you reach 150 with a word still in progress, the system lets you finish it — up to 160 characters. That extra space is the Grace zone. You will see the counter turn red and show “(Grace)” as a reminder to wrap up.

Shared links last 36 hours. The card image and metadata are stored temporarily. Download the card as a PNG if you want to keep it forever.

When a shared link expires, the landing page is no longer accessible. You can always create a new card with the same message. Downloaded PNGs stay on your device permanently.

Shared card links expire automatically after 36 hours. There is no manual delete button because card data is already temporary. If you need something removed sooner, email us.

Yes. After creating a card, you can apply any of the 6 tone styles to rewrite your message. Each tone generates a new version while keeping the original text intact.
